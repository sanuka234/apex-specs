The Skyline Bloodline

The R35 dropped the historical "Skyline" name badge from its title, but it inherited the ultimate crown. Standing side-by-side with its purist manual ancestor—the R34 Skyline GT-R—you can clearly trace the evolution of the signature quad round tail lights that define the rear view of a true Godzilla.
