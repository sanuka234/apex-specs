— THE LEGACY OF GAZOO RACING —

THE ORIGIN IDEA

After a 17-year global hiatus, Toyota Chief Engineer Tetsuya Tada was tasked with bringing back the brand's ultimate performance halo car, intending to deliver raw track precision that prioritized driving feel over simple paper specs.

THE CREATORS

Developed hand-in-hand with Toyota's elite motorsport division, GAZOO Racing (GR), the chassis was meticulously track-tested and tuned on the brutal turns of the Nürburgring Nordschleife to maximize structural rigidity.

PRODUCTION ERA

The modern MKV platform began assembly in early 2019 at Magna Steyr in Graz, Austria. It continues to stand strong as a primary sports platform, receiving a highly requested 6-speed manual gearbox addition in late 2022.

THE COMPANY

First established as an experimental in-house racing team, Toyota GAZOO Racing has evolved into a global powerhouse, conquering the World Rally Championship (WRC) and Le Mans to inject racing engineering directly into consumer keys.
